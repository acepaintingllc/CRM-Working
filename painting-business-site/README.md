# Painting Business Starter (WordPress Theme)

This folder contains a complete, minimal WordPress theme you can upload and use.

## Install

1. Zip the `painting-business-site` folder.
2. In WordPress admin, go to `Appearance > Themes > Add New > Upload Theme`.
3. Activate the theme.
4. Set your site name in `Settings > General`.
5. Add your business logo and brand colors in `style.css`/template text.

## Edit contact details

Open:

- `footer.php` to update phone/email.
- `index.php` to update pricing, services, gallery images and copy.
- `functions.php` for menus/theme options.

## Contact form

The built-in contact form is a front-end placeholder. Connect it to your contact plugin
or email integration for production handling:
- WPForms / Contact Form 7 shortcode in the contact card
- HubSpot form embed
- Custom PHP handler (security and spam protection required)
